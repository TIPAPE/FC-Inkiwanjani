About FC Inkiwanjani

Club History

Founded in 2010, FC Inkiwanjani, known as "The Wolves," has become the pride of Mile 46 and surrounding communities. Our journey from humble beginnings to becoming a competitive force in regional football is a testament to the passion and dedication of our players, management, and devoted fans.

The club was established by local football enthusiasts who shared a vision of creating a team that would represent Mile 46 with honor and excellence. Over the years, we've built a reputation for developing young talent and playing attractive, attacking football that embodies the spirit of the wolf - strong, united, and relentless.
